About Us 

Do you have a strong desire for some delectable cultural cuisine? Perhaps you're craving a delicious steak? Whatever type of dish you have in mind, The ගම්මානය Restaurant can create it for you. The ගම්මානය Restaurant has been the go-to diner for Binghamton locals since 1973. Our diner provides breakfast all day, as well as healthful and delectable lunch and supper alternatives. At The ගම්මානය Restaurant, you'll discover a variety of substantial dishes served daily, from burgers to salads, seafood to pastas. In addition, our restaurant includes a full bakery with delectable baked products and other sweets, including our famous cheesecake. Doesn't it sound delicious?
