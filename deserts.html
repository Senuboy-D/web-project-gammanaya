Watalappan is a delicious, creamy coconut custard sweetened with traditional “Kitul Jaggery,” and with warming spices like cardamom and nutmeg. Jaggery is basically a hardened block of sugar, and almost everywhere else in the world it is made with cane sugar. In Sri Lanka however, jaggery does NOT come from cane sugar.
